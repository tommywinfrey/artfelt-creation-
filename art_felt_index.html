    HELP FUND ART PROGRAMS

    With every purchase made at Artfelt Creation we will donate a portion to fund art programs in schools.

    At Artfelt Creation we believe in giving back. Artfelt Creation is the vision of Tommy Winfrey, an incarcerated artist in the California prison system. After years of imprisonment and rehabilitation, Tommy found he had a problem when he wanted to continue his higher education. He did not have the finances it required to obtain his goal of a Bachelors Degree, so he searched for a way to secure the money he needed. He turned to the one way he believed he could finance his dream, his artwork. There are existing platforms that can facilitate the selling of artwork such as EBay and Etsy; however, these channels require the assistance of an independent agent acting on the incarcerated artist's behalf. Thus Artfelt Creation was born.

    The incarcerated artist is not the only one who benefits from their unique talents. A portion of each sale will be donated to support art programs for children. Many incarcerated artists harmed their communities, and as an offender Tommy believes he and fellow offenders have an obligation to help repair some of the damage they inflicted. Tommy realizes the impact art has had on his life, and he believes there is a need for youth to discover and value their creative talents when they are young. He believes that by teaching youth to be creative through art programs, lessons can transcend into creative problem solving skills that will serve the youth in their future. In this way the incarcerate artist can be part of the solution instead of part of the problem.

    Many e-commerce sites are about making a profit, but at Artfelt Creation it is about making a difference in the world. This takes the help of everyone involved from the artist to the customer. Everyone plays an important part in the success of the community. By helping each other, we are in fact, helping ourselves. So join the community at Artfelt Creation, and help provide opportunity through creativity.
